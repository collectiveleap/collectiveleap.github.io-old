About Collective Leap

Focused on the creation of Leap, a pattern language for the design, build and operation of teams that intend to create new impact.

Leap is sourced from extensive direct and shared experiences with teams running various forms of entrepreneurship, including Lean Startup, Customer Development, Lean UX, Growth Hacking and similar.
