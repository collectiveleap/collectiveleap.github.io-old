Collective Leap

... is based on the thesis that it is possible to make intangible knowledge about human systems visible and actionable to a wide audience.

The current focus in on Impact, a pattern language to design, build and run ventures that make a difference. Impact captures entrpreneurship patterns that span multiple contexts, e.g. from early-stage to legacy businesses, individual teams to complete organizations. Sourced from extensive direct and shared experiences, with influences that include Lean Startup, Customer Development, Lean UX, Growth Hacking and similar.
